The revolution is here.

Backpack Agenda is the perfect paperless school agenda for any student or teacher's needs. Simply download the app, add your assignments, and never miss another assignment, test, quiz, event or any other thing that you encounter in your school life.

Features

Stop forgetting to do your homework! Backpack Agenda offers tons of features that make your life as student, easier!

Works everywhere

Backpack Agenda works completely offline, so there is no need to worry about missing those assignments!

Notifications

Backpack Agenda lets you set your own notifications which makes sure that you are always in control.

Customizable

Backpack Agenda lets you customize your own classes, colors, notifications and more!

Simple

With a simple calendar-like agenda and easy to add interface, you won't need to struggle with learning to use the app.

Easy

Never miss another assignment as adding the assignment when you are assigned it is simple, easy and fast.

Classes

Add and color code your own classes to fit your needs.

Full Calendar & Agenda

With Backpack Agenda, view all of your upcoming assignments in the calendar, or view see exactly what you have and when in the agenda.
